About Skill India

Skill India is a national initiative aimed at providing skills training and development opportunities to Indian citizens. Our mission is to empower the workforce of India with the skills they need to succeed in the modern economy.

We work with a variety of partners, including government agencies, private companies, and non-profit organizations, to provide training and employment opportunities in a wide range of industries. Our programs are designed to meet the needs of both individuals and employers, and we are committed to helping our trainees find meaningful and sustainable employment.

Since our launch in 2024, we have trained millions of people across the country and have helped to create a more skilled and competitive workforce. We are proud of the impact we have had on the lives of so many Indians, and we are dedicated to continuing our work in the years to come.
